Sidedefs

A sidedef is a strucuture wich describes the texture's properties of wall.

Structure

Unlike the original titles, the Doom 64 map may refers to a texture by its index (determined by his order in wad lumps), the index 65535 is reserved to no texture.

Offset (bytes) Size (bytes) C type Description
0 2 int16_t X offset of texture
2 2 int16_t Y offset of texture
4 2 int16_t Index of upper texture
6 2 int16_t Index of lower texture
8 2 int16_t Index of middle texture
10 2 int16_t Wich sector this sidedef 'faces'
