Segs

Segs is a abbreviation for segments and represents the structure part of a subsector. The segments are a result of a BSP generation by multiples splits in the map (did by a map editor), each segment is owned by a single subsector, linedef and sidedef.

Structure

The direction field can have two values: 0 if the segment runs along the front side (or "right") of the linedef or 1 if the segment runs along the back side (or "left") of the linedef.

Offset (bytes) Size (bytes) C type Description
0 2 int16_t First vertex index
2 2 int16_t Second vertex index
4 2 int16_t Angle, of full circle -32768 to 32767
6 2 int16_t Linedef index
8 2 int16_t Direction, 0 is same as the line and 1 the opposite
10 2 int16_t Offset in the linedef (distance between first vertex of seg and linedef)
